# Learning Outcomes

TCX2005 explores the strategic role of information systems in an organisation and how information systems can lead to organisational improvements in operations, planning, and decision-making. Students will learn to identify strategic opportunities for information systems deployment in an organisation and how to manage IT and lead digital transformations.

## Key Learning Outcomes

- Able to identify opportunities for IT systems deployment.
- Have an appreciation of the importance of IT in an organisation.
- Be aware of issues related to IT resource management and evaluation of IT investment.
- Develop an understanding of how IT innovation can lead to strategic advantages in competitive areas.
