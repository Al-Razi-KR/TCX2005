# MUST Read: Course Rules & Policies

## Email Communication

- Use only your official NUS email (`@u.nus.edu` or `@comp.nus.edu.sg`) for communication with teaching staff.
- Use email only for private matters.
- Prefix all email subjects with **[TCX2005]**.

## Discussions and Plagiarism

- Discussions among students are encouraged, but each student must write their own work and credit others appropriately.
- NUS has a strict academic honesty policy — read [Preventing Plagiarism](https://www.nus.edu.sg/registrar/academic-information-policies/undergraduate-students/plagiarism) on the school website.
- **No mercy policy:** both the student who copied and the student who allowed copying are penalised equally.
- Current NUS penalty for plagiarism: **F for the module without the possibility of withdrawal**. See the Student Code of Conduct.

## Absence from Assessments & Medical Matters

- If you know you will be absent from a scheduled assessment (quiz, in-class presentation), notify the Lecturer as early as possible. Unexcused absences may result in mark reduction, other penalties, or a zero.
- In the event of an excused absence, check Canvas to catch up on missed materials.
- If absent due to illness, a **medical certificate must be submitted via email within TWO working days**. The certificate must clearly state the dates you were unable to attend.
- Last-minute absences due to office work, traffic, etc. are **not** valid excuses.
- Alternative arrangements for missed assessments may be made at the Lecturer's discretion with valid documentation; failure to provide documentation results in a zero.
