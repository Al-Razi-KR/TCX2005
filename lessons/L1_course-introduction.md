## Project Briefing

**Theme:** Digital Transformation at Work

**Goal:** Identify a manual process in your workplace and redesign it using digital technologies.

### Focus Areas
1. Understand current process **(As-Is)**
2. Map user journey and pain points
3. Propose a digitally transformed process **(To-Be)**
4. Highlight impact, tech enablers, constraints & requirements

### Team Rules
- 5–6 members per team; no individual projects
- Register on Canvas → People → Project Group by **Week 2**
- No changes permitted after **Week 3 (28 May)**

### Suggested Workflow
- Identify a process from current or past workplace
- Map As-Is process using swimlanes or flowcharts
- Capture user journey and key stakeholders
- Design To-Be process with digital tools (AI, apps, IS systems)
- Specify constraints & requirements (budget, tech, change mgmt.)
- Comment on possible impact of the proposed solution

### Proposal (10%)
- Format: 2 slides (cover and references not counted)
- 1 submission per team on Canvas
- Due: Week 3

### Presentation (20%)
- Format: Up to 8 slides (cover and references not counted)
- Duration: 10 mins presentation + 5 mins Q&A
- Mode: Online via Zoom, Weeks 7–8
- Required: Visuals (process diagrams, flowcharts, etc.)
- Slides due: **Week 6 Sunday midnight (21 Jun 2026)**
