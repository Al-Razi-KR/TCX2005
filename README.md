**Textbook:** *Management Information Systems: Managing the Digital Firm*, 17th Ed. — Laudon & Laudon

---

## Learning Outcomes

This course explores the strategic role of information systems in organisations and how IS can improve operations, planning, and decision-making.

- Identify opportunities for IT systems deployment
- Appreciate the importance of IT in an organisation
- Understand IT resource management and evaluation of IT investment
- Understand how IT innovation leads to strategic competitive advantages
